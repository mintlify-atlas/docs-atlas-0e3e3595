# Installation

CashCat is available on multiple platforms so you can manage your budget wherever you are. Choose the platform that works best for you.

## Web application

The fastest way to get started is through the web application. No installation required.

### Access the web app

1. Visit [cashcat.app](https://cashcat.app) in any modern browser
2. Sign up or log in to your account
3. Start budgeting immediately

### Supported browsers

CashCat works best on:
- Chrome (version 90+)
- Firefox (version 88+)
- Safari (version 14+)
- Edge (version 90+)

<Note>
The web app is fully responsive and works on mobile browsers, but we recommend installing the native mobile apps for the best experience on phones and tablets.
</Note>

## iOS application

Get CashCat on your iPhone or iPad for on-the-go budgeting.

### Requirements

- iOS 13.0 or later
- iPhone, iPad, or iPod touch

### Installation steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download from the App Store">
    Search for "CashCat" in the App Store. The app is currently in early access.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Install the app">
    Tap **Get** and authenticate with Face ID, Touch ID, or your Apple ID password.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open and sign in">
    Launch CashCat and sign in with your existing account or create a new one.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
CashCat for iOS is built with Capacitor, ensuring a native experience while maintaining feature parity with the web version.
</Note>

## Android application

Use CashCat on Android phones and tablets.

### Requirements

- Android 5.0 (Lollipop) or later
- Google Play Services

### Installation steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download from Google Play">
    Search for "CashCat" in the Google Play Store. The app is currently in early access.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Install the app">
    Tap **Install** and wait for the download to complete.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open and sign in">
    Launch CashCat and sign in with your existing account or create a new one.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Progressive Web App (PWA)

You can also install CashCat as a Progressive Web App directly from your browser for an app-like experience without going through an app store.

### On mobile browsers

**iOS (Safari):**
1. Visit [cashcat.app](https://cashcat.app) in Safari
2. Tap the **Share** button
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**
4. Name it "CashCat" and tap **Add**

**Android (Chrome):**
1. Visit [cashcat.app](https://cashcat.app) in Chrome
2. Tap the menu button (three dots)
3. Tap **Install app** or **Add to Home Screen**
4. Confirm the installation

### On desktop browsers

**Chrome, Edge, or Brave:**
1. Visit [cashcat.app](https://cashcat.app)
2. Look for the install icon in the address bar (usually a computer with a down arrow)
3. Click it and select **Install**

The PWA will open in its own window and appear in your applications menu.

## Data synchronization

All your budget data is automatically synchronized across all platforms in real-time. This means you can:

- Start a budget on your computer
- Log transactions on your phone
- Review reports on your tablet

Your data is always up to date, no matter which device you use.

<Note>
CashCat requires an internet connection to sync data. If you're offline, you can still use the app, and your changes will sync automatically when you reconnect.
</Note>
