# Quickstart

This guide will take you from signup through creating your first budget category and logging your first transaction. By the end, you'll understand the core workflow of zero-based budgeting in CashCat.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Visit [cashcat.app](https://cashcat.app) and sign up with your email address. CashCat uses secure authentication to protect your financial data.
    
    Once you verify your email, you'll be taken to your budget dashboard.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set up your accounts">
    When you first open CashCat, you'll see the onboarding overlay. Click **"Create Your Budget!"** to begin.
    
    The Account Modal will open automatically. Here you'll add your financial accounts (checking, savings, credit cards, etc.).
    
    For each account:
    - Enter the account name (e.g., "Main Checking")
    - Set the current balance
    - Choose the account type
    
    <Note>
    Don't worry about being perfect here. You can always edit or add accounts later from the settings menu.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create your first budget category">
    After closing the Account Modal, the Manage Budget Modal will open in wizard mode.
    
    This is where zero-based budgeting begins. Create categories for how you want to spend your money this month:
    
    - **Groceries** — for food shopping
    - **Rent** — for housing costs
    - **Entertainment** — for fun activities
    - **Transportation** — for gas or transit
    
    For each category:
    1. Enter a category name
    2. Set a monthly budget amount
    3. Click **Add** or press Enter
    
    The goal is to assign all your income until you have zero dollars unassigned. This ensures every penny has a purpose.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Log your first transaction">
    Now that you have accounts and categories, you're ready to track spending.
    
    When you make a purchase:
    1. Open the transaction entry form on your budget dashboard
    2. Enter the amount you spent
    3. Select the category (e.g., "Groceries")
    4. Choose which account the money came from
    5. Add a description (optional but helpful)
    6. Save the transaction
    
    Your budget will automatically update. The category will show how much you've spent and how much remains for the month.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Stay on track">
    The core workflow is simple:
    
    - **At the start of each month:** Assign all your income into categories
    - **Throughout the month:** Log transactions as you spend
    - **Before making purchases:** Check your category balances to stay within budget
    
    Unspent money in categories will automatically roll forward to future months, so you never lose progress toward your goals.
  </Step>
</Steps>
